Outside Research

Before research I spent most of my life on a soccer field. I still play whenever I can, still watch more matches than I should, and still think the discipline of team sport is one of the better lenses for thinking about how to do hard things.

Soccer

I played professional soccer in Israel, earning promotion to the top division with three different clubs — Maccabi Netanya, Hapoel Hadera, and Hapoel Kfar Saba. I also represented the Israel National Team, and played Division 1 soccer at UNC Chapel Hill.
