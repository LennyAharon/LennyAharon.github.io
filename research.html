Research Interests

My research sits at the intersection of 3D computer vision, probabilistic modeling, and generative AI — building models that recover 3D structure from raw video, reason about uncertainty, and remain usable in real scientific workflows. I work on multi-view vision transformers, variance-inflated Ensemble Kalman Smoothers for 3D pose estimation, and open-source pipelines like Lightning Pose that minimize labeled-data burdens for behavioral analysis. I'm also actively working on mechanistic interpretability and continual learning.
